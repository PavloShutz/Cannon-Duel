IT'S A QUICK GUIDE ON HOW TO PLAY THIS GAME

1. Goal
	* Destroy opponent's fighter
	* Stay alive
	* Get some fun :)

2. Controls
	* F5 - toggle fullscreen mode
	* ESC - exit game
	* W, A - move up/down (left player)
	* Arrows UP/DOWN - move up/down (right player)
	* D - shoot (left player)
	* Arrow LEFT - shoot (right player)
